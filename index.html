Carbon Level Assesor - the saviour of your aging vehicle

Our Vision

We aim to reduce vehicle emissions with a practical, affordable solution, helping protect the environment without requiring expensive overhauls or complete transitions to electric vehicles.

How It Works

Using MQ-7 gas sensors and NRF24L01 modules, the device wirelessly transmits real-time CO data. It calculates a custom pollution index based on engine parameters, issuing alerts when emissions exceed safe levels.

Why It’s Needed

Older vehicles contribute significantly to pollution. While electric vehicles offer future solutions, this device addresses immediate emission concerns, providing an affordable way to monitor and reduce pollution from current vehicles.

Our Device

This affordable device, attaches to the vehicle’s exhaust system. Equipped with an MQ-7 gas sensor and Arduino boards, it detects harmful emissions and is housed in a heat-resistant 3D-printed case for durability.
